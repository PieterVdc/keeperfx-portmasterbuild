## Notes

KeeperFX is an open-source remake and fan expansion of Dungeon Keeper.
The main goal of KeeperFX is to preserve and expand upon the original Dungeon Keeper experience, offering many new features, improvements, and much more while staying true to the original feeling of the game
ou can delve into the original campaign, or enjoy custom content created by the community and shared on the Workshop."

## Controls

| Button | Action |
|--|--| 
|A|shift|
|B|control|
|X|Zoom to fight|
|Y|Zoom to Heart|
|select|go to next level|
|start|pause and open menu|
|LT1|Right mouse button|
|RT1|Left Mouse button|
|LT2|switch menu tab fwd/ switch spell in possession|
|RT2|switch menu tab back/ switch spell in possession  |
|RT2|switch menu tab back/ switch spell in possession  |
|right stick|move mouse|
|left stick|move camera|
|dpad|snap to button|
