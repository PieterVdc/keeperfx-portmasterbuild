This directory will store music in OGG format.

When installing KeeperFX with the keeperfx-launcher-qt.exe this folder should have been filled with the required music files.
If that failed, download it here instead: https://keeperfx.net/workshop/item/393/keeperfx-music

The music can also be extracted from the original CD or copied from the gog release. 

It is also possible to have the music play from the original CD, in that case use the "-cd" command line option while starting KeeperFX or check the option in the launcher.
