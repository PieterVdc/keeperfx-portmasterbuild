Here are my 7 post AK maps. They are to be played from within
the Deeper Dungeons add-on. Map names are hints (mostly). To
win the levels, you'll have to study psychological profile of
the Tentacles, find out how to clone Avatars, learn new ways
of utilizing enemy creatures as well as discover surprising
things about certain kind of walls. I didn't bother to add
any text messages to the scripts, so you won't receive any
hints from the game itself. But feel free to ask me, if you
have any problems with these maps.

Levels:
190, Armageddon
191, Collect
192, Sacrifice
193, Defection
194, Imps
195, Clone
196, Dig
